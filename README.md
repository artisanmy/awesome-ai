## Foundations & Research

- [Arxiv CS.AI](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/recent) - Daily preprints covering artificial intelligence research across all subfields.
- [DeepMind Research](https://deepmind.google/research/) - Published papers and discoveries from Google DeepMind spanning reinforcement learning, protein structure, and beyond.
- [OpenAI Research](https://openai.com/research) - Technical reports and papers from OpenAI on language models, alignment, and safety.
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/) - Free AI-powered research tool for exploring academic papers with citation graphs.

## Large Language Models (LLMs)

- [Claude](https://www.anthropic.com/claude) - Family of large language models from Anthropic, designed with a focus on safety and helpfulness.
- [Gemini](https://deepmind.google/technologies/gemini/) - Google DeepMind's multimodal large language model family available in multiple sizes.
- [GPT-4](https://openai.com/gpt-4) - OpenAI's large multimodal model capable of accepting image and text inputs to produce text outputs.
- [LLaMA](https://llama.meta.com/) - Meta's open-weight large language model series available for research and commercial use.
- [Mistral](https://mistral.ai/) - Open and commercial large language models from Mistral AI, known for efficiency and strong performance.

## Image & Vision

- [DALL-E 3](https://openai.com/dall-e-3) - OpenAI's text-to-image model integrated into ChatGPT and the API for generating detailed images from prompts.
- [Midjourney](https://www.midjourney.com/) - AI image generation service accessible via Discord, known for high-quality artistic outputs.
- [Stable Diffusion](https://stability.ai/stable-diffusion) - Open-weight latent diffusion model for text-to-image generation from Stability AI.

## Audio & Speech

- [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io/) - AI voice synthesis platform supporting text-to-speech, voice cloning, and dubbing in multiple languages.
- [OpenAI Whisper](https://openai.com/research/whisper) - Open-source automatic speech recognition model trained on large-scale multilingual audio data.
- [Suno](https://suno.com/) - AI music generation platform that creates full songs with vocals and instrumentation from text prompts.

## Video

- [Kling AI](https://klingai.com/) - AI video generation model from Kuaishou capable of creating realistic video clips from text and images.
- [Runway](https://runwayml.com/) - Creative AI platform offering video generation, editing, and visual effects tools for creators.
- [Sora](https://openai.com/sora) - OpenAI's text-to-video model capable of generating high-quality short videos from text descriptions.

## Multimodal

- [Claude Vision](https://www.anthropic.com/claude) - Anthropic's Claude models with vision capabilities for analyzing images alongside text.
- [Gemini 1.5 Pro](https://deepmind.google/technologies/gemini/pro/) - Google's multimodal model with a long context window supporting text, images, audio, video, and code.
- [GPT-4o](https://openai.com/gpt-4o) - OpenAI's flagship omni model with native support for text, audio, and image inputs and outputs.

## AI Coding & Developer Tools

- [Claude Code](https://www.anthropic.com/claude-code) - Agentic coding tool from Anthropic that operates directly in the terminal for software engineering tasks.
- [Copilot](https://github.com/features/copilot) - GitHub's AI pair programmer that offers code completions and chat assistance across popular editors.
- [Cursor](https://www.cursor.com/) - AI-first code editor built on VS Code with deep model integration for writing and refactoring code.
- [Devin](https://devin.ai/) - Autonomous AI software engineer from Cognition capable of completing end-to-end development tasks.

## Agents & Automation

- [AutoGen](https://microsoft.github.io/autogen/) - Microsoft framework for building multi-agent LLM applications with conversational patterns.
- [LangGraph](https://langchain-ai.github.io/langgraph/) - Library for building stateful, multi-actor agentic applications using graph-based orchestration.
- [OpenAI Agents SDK](https://openai.github.io/openai-agents-python/) - Python SDK from OpenAI for building production-ready agentic workflows with tool use and handoffs.

## AI APIs & Platforms

- [Anthropic API](https://www.anthropic.com/api) - API access to Claude models for integrating conversational AI into applications.
- [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/) - Browser-based IDE for prototyping with Gemini models and generating API keys.
- [Groq](https://groq.com/) - Inference platform offering fast LLM API access powered by custom LPU hardware.
- [OpenAI API](https://platform.openai.com/) - API platform providing access to GPT models, embeddings, DALL-E, Whisper, and more.
- [Together AI](https://www.together.ai/) - Cloud platform for running and fine-tuning open-source AI models at scale.

## Frameworks & Libraries

- [Haystack](https://haystack.deepset.ai/) - Open-source Python framework for building production-ready LLM applications and RAG pipelines.
- [LangChain](https://www.langchain.com/) - Framework for developing applications powered by large language models through composable components.
- [LlamaIndex](https://www.llamaindex.ai/) - Data framework for building LLM-powered applications over custom data sources.
- [Transformers](https://huggingface.co/docs/transformers) - Hugging Face library providing thousands of pretrained models for NLP, vision, and audio tasks.

## MLOps & Deployment

- [BentoML](https://www.bentoml.com/) - Open-source platform for building, shipping, and scaling AI applications and model serving.
- [MLflow](https://mlflow.org/) - Open-source platform for managing the ML lifecycle including tracking experiments, packaging code, and deploying models.
- [Weights & Biases](https://wandb.ai/) - Platform for experiment tracking, model versioning, and dataset management throughout the ML lifecycle.

## Data & Datasets

- [Common Crawl](https://commoncrawl.org/) - Open repository of web crawl data containing petabytes of raw web page data used to train many LLMs.
- [Hugging Face Datasets](https://huggingface.co/datasets) - Hub hosting thousands of ready-to-use datasets for NLP, vision, and multimodal tasks.
- [Papers With Code](https://paperswithcode.com/datasets) - Free resource linking machine learning papers to code implementations and benchmark datasets.

## Prompt Engineering

- [Anthropic Prompt Library](https://www.anthropic.com/prompt-library) - Curated collection of prompts optimized for Claude across a variety of use cases.
- [Learn Prompting](https://learnprompting.org/) - Free, open-source course covering prompt engineering techniques from basic to advanced.
- [OpenAI Prompt Engineering Guide](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/prompt-engineering) - Official guide from OpenAI on strategies and tactics for getting better results from language models.
- [Prompt Hub](https://www.prompthub.us/) - Community platform for discovering, sharing, and testing prompts across multiple LLM providers.

## AI Safety & Ethics

- [AI Safety Fundamentals](https://aisafetyfundamentals.com/) - Free online courses covering AI alignment and governance topics developed by BlueDot Impact.
- [Alignment Forum](https://www.alignmentforum.org/) - Research forum dedicated to technical and conceptual work on AI alignment.
- [Anthropic Safety Research](https://www.anthropic.com/safety) - Published safety research and policy work from Anthropic on responsible AI development.
- [Partnership on AI](https://partnershiponai.org/) - Multi-stakeholder organization developing responsible AI practices and publishing research on AI impacts.

## Learning Resources

- [DeepLearning.AI](https://www.deeplearning.ai/) - Platform offering courses on deep learning, LLMs, and MLOps created by Andrew Ng and collaborators.
- [fast.ai](https://www.fast.ai/) - Practical deep learning courses and a high-level library designed to make deep learning accessible.
- [Google Machine Learning Crash Course](https://developers.google.com/machine-learning/crash-course) - Free, self-paced course from Google introducing core machine learning concepts with TensorFlow.

## Communities & Newsletters

- [AI Breakfast](https://aibreakfast.beehiiv.com/) - Weekly newsletter covering the latest AI news, tools, and research developments.
- [Hugging Face Discord](https://discord.com/invite/hugging-face-879548962464493619) - Active community server for Hugging Face users discussing models, datasets, and open-source AI.
- [TLDR AI](https://tldr.tech/ai) - Daily newsletter summarizing the most important AI research, products, and industry news.

## Blogs & Podcasts

- [Latent Space](https://www.latent.space/) - Podcast and newsletter interviewing AI engineers and researchers on technical AI topics.
- [Lex Fridman Podcast](https://lexfridman.com/podcast/) - Long-form interviews with researchers, engineers, and thinkers at the forefront of AI and technology.
- [The Gradient](https://thegradient.pub/) - Publication featuring in-depth articles and interviews on AI research and its societal implications.
